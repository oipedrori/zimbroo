PRIVACY POLICY AND DATA PROTECTION - ZIMBROO APP

The user's privacy ("user", "you", "your") is Zimbroo App's number one priority ("Zimbroo", "we", "us", "our"). This policy describes how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when using our AI-assisted financial management application.

1. DATA COLLECTION
Zimbroo collects only the information necessary to operate the service:
A) Authentication: When logging in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and Google user ID.
B) Financial Transactions: We collect data entered manually or via audio, including description, value, date, and category.
C) Notion Integration: We collect integration tokens and database IDs that the user explicitly configures.

2. USE OF INFORMATION
The information is used exclusively to:
A) Process transactions and generate personalized financial reports.
B) Personalize suggestions via Artificial Intelligence (Google Gemini).
C) Synchronize data between your devices via Google Cloud (Firebase).
D) Ensure secure access to your account.

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY
All data is stored on the Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) infrastructure in the United States of America, following international security standards. Access to your data is restricted to you through your Google authentication. Zimbroo does not share, sell, or transfer your financial information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. SHARING WITH THIRD PARTIES
We operate via 3rd party APIs for specific functionalities:
A) Google Gemini API: Text data is processed by the AI for categorization and analysis.
B) Notion API: Only when configured, we read data from your database on Notion.
We do not share data with government agencies or banking institutions.

5. YOUR RIGHTS (LGPD/GDPR)
You have the following rights regarding your data:
A) Access: The right to view all your data at any time.
B) Correction: The right to edit any transaction or data.
C) Deletion: The right to permanently delete your account and all associated data. Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered.

6. COOKIES AND LOCAL DATA
We use local storage in the browser to save preferences for theme (light/dark), language, and currency, aim for a smoother user experience.

7. CHANGES TO THE POLICY
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. We will publish any changes on this page and notify users through the application interface.

8. CONTACT AND SUPPORT
For any questions or exercises of your privacy rights, contact us through the official support channels of the application.
